Welcome To The Wall!
The Wall is a digital notice board for OU students, faculty, and affilaites. Similar to the note boards around campus, but without the clutter and time inefficiency.


How to submit a post:
- Click the "SubmitPost" link on the navigation bar above. Then fill out the form with the neccesary information and your post will be reviewed by administration shortly.

How to review posts by others:
- Click "TheWall" link on the navigation bar above. The most current posts will be displayed there. You can also naviagte to "Categories" via the navigation bar and browse category speficic posts via the drop down menu.

For more info or assistance:
- Click "AboutUs" on the navigation bar above for information about the website, assistance, or to contact website adminstration.
